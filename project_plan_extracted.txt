1.  PROJECT  OVERVIEW  
Background  
In  colleges,  students  often  face  issues  related  to  classrooms,  laboratories,  
hostels,
 
libraries,
 
internet
 
connectivity,
 
electrical
 
facilities,
 
and
 
other
 
campus
 
infrastructure.
 
Complaints
 
are
 
usually
 
communicated
 
through
 
informal
 
channels,
 
making
 
it
 
difficult
 
to
 
track
 
their
 
status
 
and
 
resolution.
 
The  Campus  Complaint  Management  System  (CCMS)  provides  a  centralized  
platform
 
where
 
students
 
can
 
raise
 
complaints,
 
track
 
their
 
status,
 
and
 
receive
 
updates
 
while
 
administrators
 
can
 
manage
 
and
 
resolve
 
complaints
 
efficiently.

2.  PROJECT  OBJECTIVES  
The  objectives  of  the  project  are:  
●  Develop  a  web  application  using  the  MERN  Stack.  ●  Provide  an  online  complaint  registration  system.  ●  Enable  students  to  track  complaint  status.  ●  Allow  administrators  to  manage  complaints.  ●  Practice  frontend,  backend,  database,  and  integration  concepts.  ●  Simulate  Agile-based  project  development.
